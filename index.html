Nut Notes Privacy Policy

Thank you for using Nut Notes. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting any information you provide while using our application.

Nut Notes does not collect personal information from users. We do not request names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other identifying data. All notes created by users are stored locally on the device and are not transmitted to external servers.
